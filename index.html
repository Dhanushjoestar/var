☀ AR Solar System Explorer

How to use this app

📋
Step 1 — Get the Hiro Marker
Tap "Show Hiro Marker" after closing this. Display it on a laptop screen or print it on paper.
📱
Step 2 — Point camera at marker
Hold your phone 25–40 cm above the marker. Keep it steady. The solar system appears in 1–2 seconds.
👆
Step 3 — TAP directly on a planet
Tap precisely on the planet sphere. Larger planets (Jupiter, Saturn) are easiest to tap. A blue ripple confirms the tap was detected.
📖
Step 4 — Explore the data
An info panel slides up with 5 tabs: Overview, Atmosphere, Moons, Missions, and Key Facts. Scroll each tab for deep data.
🎛
Bottom controls
Speed slider controls orbit speed. Labels/Orbits buttons toggle what's shown. Marker button shows the Hiro marker image.
